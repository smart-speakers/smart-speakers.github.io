HT SPOT'18

The second edition of the intra school oratory, debate, impromptu and quiz contest took place at the Federal Polytechnic tagged the Internal hot spot in affiliation with the student union government.

The first edition was a much talk about event and this was the reason why the management of the Polytechnic community in conjuction with the student apex body of the institution requested for the continuity of the program in the institution.

The shortcomings of the previous Hot Spot was improved upon, hot spot'18 was fully digitalized and this was a serious achievement compared to the previous events and it was an event that will forever remain in the heart of many.

It was a three a three round contest. 1st Round was pure Oratory in the 24 departments across the five schools with quiz competition.
2nd Round was a debate competition and quiz among the qualifiers of the first round and the 3rd round was oratory, impromptu and quiz contest.

Hot Spot'19 will debut another aspect of public speaking which is known as EXCERPT. And Smart Speakers Organization is looking beyond Federal Polytechnic Nasarawa (Internal Hot Spot will still go on), we taking the the entire Polytechnic and Monotechnic by storm in Conjuction with National Association Polytechnic Students NAPS With the endorsement of NBTE and Federal Ministry of Education.
