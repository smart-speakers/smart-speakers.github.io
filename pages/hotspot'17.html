HT SPOT'17

The maiden edition of Hot Spot, a gladiatorial combat of the intellect debut in mid 2017 at Federal Polytechnic Nasarawa being the host community of Smart Speakers Organization.

5 Schools, 24 Departments, 1 Podium and 1 Winner. It was indeed a battle of wits and word.

The department of Agric and Bio-Engineering Technology under the school of Engineering emerged as the overall best in the school of engineering and the gladiator of Hot Spot'17.

Hot Spot'17 was the first intra school oratory, debate, impromptu and quiz contest in the history of The Federal Polytechnic Nasarawa, it was as a result of the success of Hot Spot'17 that lead to the birth of HOT SPOT'18. The management of FPN deemed it fit and requested for its continuity as it was the only event on campus that encompasses both social and academics. Hence the word EDUTAINMENT!

The students didn't hide their joy as they gave positive feedback and wanted more of such edutainment event on campus, and that is why we are committed to inspire and touch lives of youths and the general public by helping them discover their innate abilities.
