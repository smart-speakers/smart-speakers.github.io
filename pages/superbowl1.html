Maiden Edition

The first edition of super bowl took place in Nasarawa community with public and private schools participating in large numbers. Although the support wasn't firm because events like this was alien to them, but the intelligensia in the community recognise and appreciate it for what it was.

We have talented students across this country and these students are mostly in places where you least expected, and that is why we (SSO) are committed and we are not backing down now or never!

Educational system in the northern part of Nigeria is in a total sham, and the system has been totally bastadized mostly in the Public Schools and if this issue is not address and corrected, it will affect the future of our dear country.

Lets join forces to give basic education the much needed and deserve attention. our kids are our future.

Feel free to invite us to your community via the comment section below and we will get back to you! We inspire and touch lives..
